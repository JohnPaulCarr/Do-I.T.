# North Idaho College Do IT Club
The NIC Do IT Club is a student organization at North Idaho College that focuses on technology and computer science. 
The club provides a space for students to learn, collaborate, and work on projects related to programming, software development, and other tech-related topics.

## Regular Meetings
The club regularly meets on Wednesdays at 2:00 PM through 3:00 PM in CITE room 224G of the Hedlund building.
